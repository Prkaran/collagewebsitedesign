About Department

    The IT Department of Amrutvahini Polytechnic, Sangamner is established in year 2001 which was transforming period in IT education when computers were used in wide range of applications. Whether it is a medical, mechanical, civil, Banking, Education and electronics applications, the computers are used to solve the complex problem in efficient manner in a short time. Today computer industry needs adaptable people to represent the business solutions.

    We have well equipped computer Laboratories with LAN of sufficient numbers of computers with latest configuration, UPS, Multi-function printers, scanners, LCD Projectors etc.. We provide quality education to our students with tutor CDs, efficient staff and teaching aids.

    Students of our department have given internet facility via 40 MBPS broadband connection and 25 MBPS leased line Internet connection from BSNL. Ours students are also participating in MSBTE organized Debate competition, paper presentation, Blind C competition, Quiz etc. The department have installed various system software such as Windows 8, Windows 2008 server, Linux Mint and application softwares such as Office 2010, IBM Rational Rose, JDK 7.0, Visual Basic 6.0, and Turbo C++.

    Our department have organized one week MSBTE sponsored training program on "Multimedia and Animation technology" in year 2007-2008, G Scheme Curriculum Implementation program for subjects Microprocessor and Object Oriented Programming. Three no of NITTR online trainings in ICT mode in year 2019.

    Our staff member have also worked as a website coordinator to develop a web site www.amrutpoly.co.in and used content management system. We have timely coordinated for getting the present effects of our site. Our staff member have also worked as a coordinator for successful implementation of ERP software “ANanoSoft” used for College administration as well as Staff, Student use.
